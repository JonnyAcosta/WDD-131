Overview

Purpose

My purpose for this website is to show Mexico to people who wants to know about Mexico. I want people to learn about Mexico and gave a love for it that they will have the describe to visit it.

Audience

My audience is for people who want to learn about Mexico, People who want to visit Mexico, and for other Mexicans to understand and learn about their culture.

Dynamic elements

Some of the eynamic elements I will use in JavaScript is to have some interactive things like quizzes, maps, and event handling. I want to make a interactice map that uses addEventListener, and DOM manipulation. I want to use arrays to make like a random fact generator and to organize and display data. I will use objects to stor state details, city data and festival dates. I will also will use COnditional Branching for the quizzes, game interactions and dynamic updates.
